 in this program, there are two types of aliases 'Circle' and 'Rectangle' defined to present the shapes. which defines the properties specific to each shape 'radius' for Circle and 'width' and 'height' for Rectangle. the function 'shapeDs' takes parameter shapes which take the type 'Circle' or 'Rectangle' which means it can accept one of them. in the if condition it checks the value of 'shapes.shape' properties to find whether it's a Circle or a Rectangle.
